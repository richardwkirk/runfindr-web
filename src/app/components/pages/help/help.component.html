Navigating runfindr.uk

  • To see a menu of options select the running shoe in the header.
  • To search different countries or athletes, select the magnifying glass.
  • The athlete search allows you to compare athletes if you turn on the "Compare Athletes" switch.
  • When viewing maps you will get two icons on your map:
    1. A settings cog to change map display options.
    2. An athlete display icon to show details of the loaded athelte.

parkrun data

runfindr.uk is not associated with the parkrun organisation.

The site loads parkrun data from the parkrun websites. The data is loaded on demand through a runfindr.uk server acting as a proxy. The data is reformatted and presented in maps and athlete trump cards. No parkrun data is stored by runfindr.uk other than in internal caches used to reduce network traffic. These caches are cleared regularly.

runfindr.uk does not store your personal data. The local browser storage on your device will remember your search history and athlete data you have previously viewed. This is only stored locally only and not on the runfindr.uk server. You can clear your local storage to remove any runfindr.uk data history. Our server logs will track what requests for data are made to the parkrun website as these requests are routed through runfindr.uk servers. No data returned from the requests will be held on the server. We only record which pages were requested and when.
